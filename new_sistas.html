SISTAS Empowerment Zone

SISTAS Empowerment is a space created for women from all walks of life to come together to be themselves, to build sisterhood and create a web of support for one another. It is a space where we can uplift and assist one another to heal, and fulfill our purpose. Removing all the roadblocks and excuses that we use and learn to tap into our inner Goddess. This is a space that we can "Create an Atmosphere of Empowerment."

Learn more

VISION of SISTAS Empowerment Zone.

This is a virtual space that we can:

  • Use one another as a bridge to our healing
  • Be vulnerable and share our most heartfelt stories that may help someone else
  • Find supportive resources and compassion to help us reach our goals
  • Be supported and inspired to follow our dreams and goals
  • Confide and trust our sisters for support
  • Share our wealth of knowledge and wisdom
  • Assist and Inspire healing
  • Be empowered to overcome our obstacles
  • Celebrate each other
  • Encourage one another
  • Embrace our greatness and power
  • Find solutions and resources
