About SISTAS

SISTAS EMPOWERMENT ZONES is a social media site created by Shalonda Menefee

SISTAS Empowerment is a space created for women from all walks of life to come together to be themselves, to build sisterhood and create a web of support for one another. It is a space where we can uplift and assist one another to heal, and fulfill our purpose. Removing all the roadblocks and excuses that we use and learn to tap into our inner Goddess. This is a space that we can "Create an Atmosphere of Empowerment"

We all have a great deal of experience, knowledge and resources that may help another SISTA. This is a space for all women to connect,share ideas, and support each other in our personal growth and endeavors. But most of all to build a community of support.
