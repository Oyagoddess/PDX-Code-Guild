About SISTAS

SISTAS EMPOWERMENT ZONES is a social media site created by Shalonda Menefee

SISTAS Empowerment is a space created for women from all walks of life to come together to be themselves, to build sisterhood and create a web of support for one another. It is a space where we can uplift and assist one another to heal, and fulfill our purpose. Removing all the roadblocks and excuses we use and tap into our inner Goddess "Creating an Atmosphere of Empowerment" for ourselves and others we connect with. We all have a great deal of experience, knowledge and resources that we can impart to another. This is a space for all women to connect,share ideas, and support one another in our personal growth and endeavors. But most of all to build a community of support.

This is a virtual space that we can:

  • Use one another as a bridge to our healing
  • Be vulnerable and share our most heartfelt stories that we struggle with
  • Find supportive resources and compassion to help us move forward
  • Be supported and inspired to follow our dreams and goals
  • Confide and trust our sisters to support us through what we may feel and are going through
  • Share our wealth of knowledge and wisdom,
  • Assist and Inspire healing
  • Be empowered to overcome our obstacles
  • Celebrate each other
  • Encourage one another
  • Embrace our greatness and power
  • Find solutions and resources
  • Learn and Grow
  • And much more
